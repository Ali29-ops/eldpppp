About Us

Trusted for Quality. Driven by Innovation.

With over 25 years of experience, El Dawleya for Plastic has grown into a trusted name in Egypt and the region for delivering high-quality plastic solutions. We specialize in manufacturing precision-engineered plastic products using advanced technologies and sustainable practices to serve the needs of diverse industries — including food, cosmetics, medical, and industrial sectors.

Our commitment to innovation, reliability, and customer success drives us to create tailored solutions using PET, PE, and PP materials, ensuring both performance and safety. With a forward-thinking team, scalable production capabilities, and an unwavering focus on quality, we proudly contribute to our clients' success across local and regional markets.
